## Money-Saving Tips

1. **Buy L298N in bulk** — Binarytech has 135+ units at 1,000 DA each
2. **PVC tube locally** — plumbing stores often cheaper than Polytub list prices
3. **18650 batteries on Ouedkniss** — compare multiple sellers for best price
4. **AliExpress for sensors** — VL53L0X and MPX5010DP are much cheaper imported (~1,500 DA vs 3,000+ DA locally)
5. **Skip MPX5010DP if budget tight** — use a cheaper pressure sensor like BMP280 (~500 DA) for depth estimation (reduced accuracy)
6. **O-rings at hydraulic stores** — much cheaper than specialized electronics retailers
